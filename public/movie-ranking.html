My Favourite Movies - Michael Edition

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inception

Christopher Nolan really created a masterpiece with this, with great performances from a star-studded cast list, notably Leonardo Di Caprio, Tom Hardy, Michael Caine. I recommend.

Lion King

The word that best describes this movie would be golden, when Disney knew how to make good movies.

Full Metal Alchemist

I must say this is not a movie but a an anime series but this is one of the finest works ever, the story, the characters and the action will make you gripped on your seat for more nonstop drama!
